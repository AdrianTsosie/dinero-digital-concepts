The Company

Dinero Digital was formed 8 years ago starting as a Graphic Design firm. We have extended our services and offer Web Design and Development, Projection Mapping and Motion Graphics. At Dinero Digital we apply a unique approach to every project that will give the final product a look and style that will set your company apart from your competition

Our Services

Graphic Design

Dinero Digital designs for print, digital use, or vector art for screen printing and cut vinyl signage. We also create work for CNC and Plasma cutters. Weather you need design work for an internet campaign, business cards, flyers, brochures, posters, t-shirt designs, or design for signage we have you covered.

Web Development

Web Development is an ever-changing and evolving technology, and Dinero Digital strives to keep up with these trends. We are here to give you a fresh current look and feel to your internet presence.

Projection Mapping

Projection Mapping has quickly grown in popularity over the past 5 years. Dinero Digital was contracted by a local night club promoter to add a great visual element to parties. We set out a creative eye for building this custom installation and from this we have grown a following for being eye catching and immersive. Are you need to add a visual appealing element to your special event? Projection Mapping may be an idea for you.

Motion Graphics

Our Motion Graphic practice started 2 years ago to fill the need of clients whom needed animation of their logos and short commercials to help promote their business. The great thing about Motion Graphics is that it combines both visual and audio to evoke a strong emotion and create a solid connection between a brand, a message and an audience.
